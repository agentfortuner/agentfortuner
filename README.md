#  About Me:
I’m Krishnan Lakshmi Narayana, a filmmaker, electronics engineer, and researcher working at the intersection of technology, storytelling, and innovation.<br><br>Currently, I’m pursuing a B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering at JNTUK and a Diploma in Data Science from IIT Madras (’26). I am also a SIH’23 Finalist and the founder behind The Brash Jacks Company, and Trishul Astra Dynamics Incorp.<br><br>My work blends creative direction with technical problem-solving, spanning areas like AI-driven applications, robotics, aerospace-oriented systems, and digital media production. Through my ventures, I focus on building platforms that merge cinema, technology, and research into scalable, real-world solutions.<br><br>As the founder of The Brash Jacks Company, I lead initiatives across film, television, publishing, and interactive media, aiming to create content that is both innovative and impactful. My interests also extend into linguistics, intelligent systems, and experimental tech, where I explore how technology can enhance human expression and understanding.
